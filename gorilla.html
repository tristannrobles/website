Gorilla Cumbia (Unfinished Project)

After composing a psychedelic cumbia, I came up with the idea of making a quirky animation about a gorilla who always appears tough, but all he really wants is to go home and compose the music he truly loves. In the small teaser, I combined different animation techniques, implementing vector animation, traditional animation, and 3D.
