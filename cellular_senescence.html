Cellular Senescence

To fulfill my community service requirement during my university education, I was invited to participate in a collaborative project in support of the Medicine program at the University Center of Health Sciences, also part of the UDG network. The task was to create a Motion Graphics video addressing the topic assigned to the volunteer. In my case, I had to talk about Cellular Senescence. Fortunately, I was assigned a team of very enthusiastic Medicine students who conducted the research and wrote a script. From this script, we developed a visually aesthetic universe inspired by Kurzgesagt's educational videos. The use of a dreamy and luminous palette evokes relaxing sensations that accompany you throughout the almost 9-minute duration.
