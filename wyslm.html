WOULD YOU STILL LOVE ME?

Based on the age-old question: "If I were a little worm, would you still love me?" I decided to create this series of animations in Blender where I explore the tenderest side of the enigma. Are there limits to love? Could the way you love me change? What would the new dynamics of the relationship be? This project helped me explore the capabilities of Blender, experimenting with the tools I have learned so far.
