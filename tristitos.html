Tristitos Fanzine

Tristitos is a fanzine that was created as an attempt to generate a creative community within my university and also to promote the sale of self-managed publications within a space occupied by those who wish to spread their spark with others. It doesn't have a fixed theme; it's just an excuse to illustrate and share ideas I have at the moment. It serves as an alternative to posting on social media and represents an approach to the printed medium.

You can read all the Tristitos by clicking on their cover.
