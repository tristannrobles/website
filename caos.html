CAOS (Pt. 2) Lyric Video

In this project, I created a lyric video for a song with a post-apocalyptic theme. The typography and background texture, composed of a wall of "hell-digital" numbers, play a crucial role in conveying the song's dark and desolate tone. The choice of these visual elements enhances the atmosphere of the video, immersing the viewer in the dystopian world described by the lyrics.
