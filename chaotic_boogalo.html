Chaotic Boogalo Title Animation

I was part of a collaborative project with amazing friends and creatives for the visuals that would be projected at the launch event for the artist Boogie's EP and the drop of the duo Radical Brighton. My contribution included animating the logo for the cover created by the incredibly talented Radical Souvenir (Maximiliano Nápoles).
